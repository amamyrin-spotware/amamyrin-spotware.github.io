Key Insights

rocket

Strategic Growth

Brokers and Challenges were the clear winners. Clicks on Brokers surged from less than 1 per day to nearly 22 per day, validating the new navigation hierarchy.

cycle

Cannibalization of More Items

Indicators dropped 59%, Bots 68%. The new structure successfully prioritized main items, redirecting user attention away from deeper sub-menu categories.

chat

Discord Decline

Discord engagement fell 38%, likely because it is now less prominent in the new navigation, being bypassed in favor of Challenges and Brokers sections.

store

Store - Early Data

Store tracking only began April 22, 2026. Its low daily average of 0.18 is expected given the very short data collection window - monitor in the next cycle.
